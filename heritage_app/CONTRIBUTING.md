# Contributing to World Heritage Hub 🌍

Thank you for your interest in the **World Heritage Hub**! We are dedicated to preserving human culture through collaboration between developers, cultural custodians, and researchers.

## Ways to Contribute

### 🛡️ Cultural Validation (Custodians & Elders)
If you are a recognized custodian of a specific culture or tradition:
1. Review pending heritage entries in your area of expertise.
2. Provide validations if the information is accurate.
3. Use the "Elder Verification" system to endorse high-quality, authentic submissions.

### 💻 Development (Developers)
- **UI/UX**: Help us refine our glassmorphic interface or improve map interactivity.
- **Features**: Implement new content type filters or enhance the analytics dashboard.
- **Bug Fixes**: Help us maintain the integrity of our cultural encyclopedia.
- **Localization**: Help us translate the platform into more local heart languages.

### 📖 Content Submission (Contributors)
- Share stories, games, medicine, recipes, crafts, and festivals from your community.
- Ensure all "Traditional Medicine" entries include the required safety disclaimers.

## Getting Started

1. Fork the repository.
2. Create a new branch: `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`.
3. Make your changes and commit them: `git commit -m 'Add some feature'`.
4. Push to the branch: `git push origin feature/your-feature-name`.
5. Open a Pull Request.

## Code of Conduct
Respect for all cultures, tribes, and traditions is our #1 rule. Hate speech, cultural appropriation, or intentional misinformation will result in immediate removal from the platform.
